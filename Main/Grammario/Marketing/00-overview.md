# Grammario marketing playbook

A prioritized growth marketing strategy for launching Grammario to the wider public. Targeted at serious intermediate-to-advanced language learners across German, Turkish, Russian, Spanish, and Italian.

## Phases at a glance

| Phase | Channel | Timeline | Priority |
|-------|---------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Community seeding | Week 1–2 | Start here |
| 2 | Creator outreach | Week 2–4 | Highest ROI |
| 3 | Short-form content engine | Week 3–6 | Scale channel |
| 4 | SEO + product-led growth | Month 2+ | Long game |

## Core positioning principle

Lead with value before the ask — on Reddit, answer three threads before mentioning Grammario. In creator outreach, reference their specific video before pitching. On TikTok, solve a real grammar pain in the first 3 seconds before showing the app.

Your target audience (serious intermediate learners) is skeptical of generic language app marketing because they've been burned by shallow tools. The positioning must signal "this is for people who are past the basics."
