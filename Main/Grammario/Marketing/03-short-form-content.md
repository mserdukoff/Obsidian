# Phase 3 — Short-form content engine

**Timeline:** Week 3–6
**Cost:** $0 organic → $20–30/day paid after validation
**Goal:** Scalable top-of-funnel awareness; eventually a repeatable paid acquisition channel

> Do not run paid ads until you have at least 2–3 organic videos with 5k+ views. Use organic to find your winning creative first.

---

## TikTok and Instagram Reels — organic content

🟡 Medium effort

### Steps

1. Create a dedicated TikTok/Reels account for Grammario. Profile bio: *"Grammar analysis for serious language learners — grammario.ai"*
2. Core content format: **screen-record yourself pasting real text into Grammario** and walking through the analysis. "I wrote this German paragraph and here's every mistake I was making."
3. Post **3x per week minimum** for the first 6 weeks. Consistency beats production quality at this stage.
4. Study what's working: search `#languagelearning`, `#germanlearning`, `#learnrussian` on TikTok — see which formats get high engagement. Mirror those structures with your own content.
5. Add a strong CTA in every video: "Link in bio — first 10 analyses free." Reduce friction to first use.

### High-performing content angles

| Angle | Hook example |
|-------|-------------|
| Duolingo contrast | "Mistakes Duolingo will never catch" |
| Error analysis | "I wrote a paragraph in German and here's everything wrong with it" |
| Rule explainer | "Why your German still sounds unnatural (even if your vocab is good)" |
| Progress proof | "6 months of grammar analysis — here's what changed" |
| Language myth | "The grammar rule everyone teaches wrong" |

### Format notes

- First 2 seconds must hook. Start with the problem, not the app.
- Show the app output on screen — the visual analysis is your product demo.
- 30–60 seconds is the sweet spot for language learning content.
- Captions/subtitles increase watch time by ~40% — use CapCut auto-captions.

## TikTok Smart+ paid ads — after organic validation

🔴 High effort

### Prerequisites

- At least 2–3 organic videos with 5,000+ views
- A landing page that matches the ad message exactly (not the generic homepage)
- Affiliate/tracking links set up (UTM parameters at minimum)

### Steps

1. Take your best-performing organic video and boost it as a Smart+ campaign. Let TikTok's algorithm find your audience.
2. **Targeting inputs:** Interests in language learning, education, travel. Age 20–40. Layer on competitor audiences (Duolingo, Babbel users).
3. **Budget:** Start at $20–30/day for 5–7 days. Evaluate cost-per-signup (CPS), not cost-per-click. If CPS is under $8, scale.
4. Test 3 creatives simultaneously. Kill the bottom two after 3 days. Run the winner.
5. Landing page must match the ad: if the ad says "analyze your German grammar free," the page says exactly that.

### Metrics to track

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Cost per click | < $0.80 |
| Cost per signup (free) | < $3.00 |
| Cost per paid conversion | < $15.00 |
| Free → paid conversion rate | > 8% |

### Scaling rule

If cost-per-paid-conversion is under $15 and your plan is $9–12/mo, you recoup ad spend in month 2. Scale the budget by 20% every 3–4 days once validated — don't double overnight or TikTok resets the learning phase.
